Hygiene Awareness

Dental Hygiene

Neglecting dental hygiene can result in cavities, gum disease, and even tooth loss. Brushing twice daily and regular dental check-ups are vital for maintaining oral health.

Personal Hygiene

Inadequate personal hygiene can lead to skin infections, body odor, and more serious health conditions. Daily bathing and clean clothing are key practices to adopt.

Food Hygiene

Improper food hygiene can cause foodborne illnesses such as salmonella and E. coli. Proper cooking, storage, and handling of food are crucial for health safety.
