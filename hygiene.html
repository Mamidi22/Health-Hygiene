Health and Hygiene

Learn how to maintain your health and hygiene for a better life.

Personal Hygiene

Personal hygiene involves those practices performed by an individual to care for one's bodily health and well-being through cleanliness. This includes regular handwashing, brushing and flossing teeth, and bathing.

Healthy Eating

A balanced diet is crucial for maintaining good health. Incorporate a variety of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains into your diet. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

Exercise

Regular physical activity helps to improve overall health and fitness. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week.
