Overview

Importance of Health and Hygiene

Good health and hygiene practices are essential for preventing diseases and promoting overall well-being. They include regular handwashing, proper nutrition, adequate sleep, and exercise.

Health and Hygiene Tips

  • Wash your hands frequently, especially before meals and after using the restroom.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Exercise regularly to stay fit and maintain a healthy weight.
  • Get enough sleep each night to recharge your body and mind.
  • Practice good oral hygiene by brushing and flossing your teeth daily.
