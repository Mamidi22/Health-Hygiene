Nutrition Guide

Fruits and Vegetables

Fruits and vegetables are essential for a healthy diet. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. Aim to include a variety of colors in your diet to get a wide range of nutrients.

Whole Grains

Whole grains provide essential nutrients and fiber. They help in maintaining a healthy weight and reducing the risk of chronic diseases. Examples include whole wheat, brown rice, oats, and quinoa.

Lean Proteins

Lean proteins are crucial for muscle repair and growth. They include poultry, fish, beans, and legumes. Opt for grilled or baked options instead of fried to keep them healthy.

Dairy

Dairy products are a great source of calcium and vitamin D, important for bone health. Choose low-fat or fat-free options such as milk, yogurt, and cheese to reduce calorie intake while still getting essential nutrients.
